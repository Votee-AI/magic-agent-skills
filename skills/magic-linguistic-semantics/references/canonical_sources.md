# Canonical Sources — magic-linguistic-semantics

Snapshot 2026-04-23.

## WordNet
- **Fellbaum, C.** (ed.) (1998). *WordNet: An Electronic Lexical Database*. MIT Press.
- **Miller, G. A.** (1995). *WordNet: A Lexical Database for English*. CACM.
- **Bond, F., & Foster, R.** (2013). *Linking and Extending an Open Multilingual Wordnet*. ACL.
- WordNet: https://wordnet.princeton.edu/
- OMW: http://compling.hss.ntu.edu.sg/omw/

## FrameNet
- **Fillmore, C. J.** (1982). *Frame Semantics*. Linguistics in the Morning Calm.
- **Baker, C. F., Fillmore, C. J., Lowe, J. B.** (1998). *The Berkeley FrameNet Project*. ACL.
- **Ruppenhofer, J., et al.** (2016). *FrameNet II: Extended Theory and Practice*.
- FrameNet: https://framenet.icsi.berkeley.edu/

## PropBank / SRL
- **Palmer, M., Gildea, D., Kingsbury, P.** (2005). *The Proposition Bank*. Computational Linguistics.
- **Hajič, J., et al.** (2009). *The CoNLL-2009 Shared Task: Syntactic and Semantic Dependencies in Multiple Languages*. CoNLL.
- **Lopez de Lacalle, M., et al.** (2014). *Predicate Matrix*. LREC.

## MWE / PARSEME
- **Sag, I. A., et al.** (2002). *Multiword Expressions: A Pain in the Neck for NLP*. CICLing.
- **Constant, M., et al.** (2017). *Multiword Expression Processing: A Survey*. Computational Linguistics.
- **Savary, A., et al.** (2017, 2018, 2020). PARSEME shared task papers.

## Foundational semantics
- **Bender, E. M., & Lascarides, A.** (2019). *Linguistic Fundamentals for NLP II: 100 Essentials from Semantics and Pragmatics*. Morgan & Claypool.
- **Cruse, D. A.** (2011). *Meaning in Language: An Introduction to Semantics and Pragmatics* (3rd ed.). OUP.
- **Saeed, J. I.** (2016). *Semantics* (4th ed.). Wiley-Blackwell.

## Lexicography (cross-reference)
- **Atkins, B. T. S., & Rundell, M.** (2008). *The Oxford Guide to Practical Lexicography*. OUP.
