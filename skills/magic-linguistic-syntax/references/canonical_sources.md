# Canonical Sources — magic-linguistic-syntax

Snapshot 2026-04-23.

## Universal Dependencies
- **Nivre, J., et al.** (2020). *Universal Dependencies v2: An Evergrowing Multilingual Treebank Collection*. LREC.
- **de Marneffe, M.-C., et al.** (2021). *Universal Dependencies*. Computational Linguistics.
- **Zeman, D., et al.** (2017, 2018). CoNLL Shared Task on Multilingual Parsing from Raw Text to UD.
- UD site: https://universaldependencies.org/

## Parser tools
- **Nguyen, M. V., et al.** (2021). *Trankit*. EACL demo.
- **Qi, P., et al.** (2020). *Stanza*. ACL.
- **Kondratyuk, D., & Straka, M.** (2019). *75 Languages, 1 Model: Parsing UD Universally* (UDify). EMNLP.
- **Honnibal, M., et al.** spaCy. https://spacy.io/

## Cross-lingual transfer
- **de Vries, W., Wieling, M., Nissim, M.** (2022). *Make the Best of Cross-lingual Transfer: Evidence from POS Tagging with Over 100 Languages*. ACL.
- **Lin, Y.-H., et al.** (2019). *Choosing Transfer Languages for Cross-Lingual Learning*. ACL.
- **Pires, T., et al.** (2019). *How Multilingual is Multilingual BERT?*. ACL.

## Agreement probes / grammatical eval
- **Marvin, R., & Linzen, T.** (2018). *Targeted Syntactic Evaluation of Language Models*. EMNLP.
- **Warstadt, A., et al.** (2020). *BLiMP*. TACL.
- **Mueller, A., et al.** (2020). *CLAMS*. ACL.
- **Hu, J., et al.** (2020). *SyntaxGym*. ACL.
- **Linzen, T., et al.** (2016). *Assessing the Ability of LSTMs to Learn Syntax-Sensitive Dependencies*. TACL.

## Foundational syntax
- **Nivre, J.** (2008). *Algorithms for Deterministic Incremental Dependency Parsing*. Computational Linguistics.
- **Carnie, A.** (2013). *Syntax: A Generative Introduction* (3rd ed.). Wiley.
- **Tallerman, M.** (2020). *Understanding Syntax* (5th ed.). Routledge.

## Refresh
- UD: annual May release; treebanks added between.
- Parser tools: Trankit + stanza follow ~6-month cycles.
- BLiMP-style benchmarks: research-driven; check ACL/EMNLP findings annually.
