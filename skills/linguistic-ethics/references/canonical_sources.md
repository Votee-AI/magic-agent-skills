## Refresh procedure

- CARE Principles: stable since 2020; re-check annually for new operationalization papers.
- FPIC: stable since UNDRIP 2007; re-check for jurisdiction-specific applications annually.
- License landscape: re-review annually (CC version updates, EU AI Act enforcement decisions, US litigation outcomes).
- Country-specific frameworks: check with community partners annually.

## A note on intent

This is a hard area. People disagree in good faith. The frameworks here are best-current-practice; they will evolve. When in doubt:
1. Default to community partnership.
2. Default to less use rather than more.
3. Default to documentation rather than ad-hoc decision.
4. Default to revisable / revocable use.

The downside of over-applying ethics is slowness. The downside of under-applying is irreversible harm.
