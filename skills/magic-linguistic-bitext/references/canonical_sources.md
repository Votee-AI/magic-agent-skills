# Canonical Sources — magic-linguistic-bitext

Snapshot 2026-04-23.

## Mining and alignment foundations

- **Costa-jussà, M. R., et al. (NLLB Team)** (2022). *No Language Left Behind: Scaling Human-Centered MT*. arXiv 2207.04672.
- **Thompson, B., & Koehn, P.** (2019). *Vecalign: Improved Sentence Alignment in Linear Time and Space*. EMNLP.
- **Heffernan, K., Çelebi, O., Schwenk, H.** (2022). *Bitext Mining Using Distilled Sentence Representations for Low-Resource Languages*. EMNLP findings.
- **Schwenk, H., et al.** (2021). *CCMatrix: Mining Billions of High-Quality Parallel Sentences on the Web*. ACL.
- **Schwenk, H., et al.** (2021). *WikiMatrix: Mining 135M Parallel Sentences in 1620 Language Pairs from Wikipedia*. EACL.
- **El-Kishky, A., et al.** (2020). *CCAligned: A Massive Collection of Cross-Lingual Web-Document Pairs*. EMNLP.

## Embedding models

- **Heffernan et al.** (2022). LASER3.
- **Duquenne et al.** (2023). *SONAR: Sentence-Level Multimodal and Language-Agnostic Representations*. arXiv.
- **Feng, F., et al.** (2022). *Language-agnostic BERT Sentence Embedding* (LaBSE). ACL.

## Synthetic bitext

- **Sennrich, R., Haddow, B., Birch, A.** (2016). *Improving Neural Machine Translation Models with Monolingual Data*. ACL.
- **Edunov, S., Ott, M., Auli, M., Grangier, D.** (2018). *Understanding Back-Translation at Scale*. EMNLP.
- **Kim, Y., Petrov, P., Petrushkov, P., Khadivi, S., Ney, H.** (2019). *Pivot-based Transfer Learning for Neural Machine Translation between Non-English Languages*. EMNLP.
- **Burchell, L., et al.** (2024). *Scaling Low-Resource MT via Synthetic Data*. (Practitioner survey.)
- **Aharoni, R., Johnson, M., Firat, O.** (2019). *Massively Multilingual Neural Machine Translation*. NAACL.

## OPUS and aggregator catalogs

- OPUS: https://opus.nlpl.eu/
- **Tiedemann, J.** (2012). *Parallel Data, Tools and Interfaces in OPUS*. LREC.

## Region-specific MT
- MasakhaneMT, MAFAND-MT (Africa), AmericasNLP MT shared tasks, IndicTrans2 (AI4Bharat), SEACrowd MT subset.

## Refresh procedure
- LASER3 stable; SONAR follows annual cycle.
- NLLB recipe + margin thresholds: re-check WMT and OLDI shared task findings annually.
- OPUS additions: monthly check for new corpora.
