# Canonical Sources — linguistic-corpus

## Catalog references
- **Costa-jussà, M. R., et al. (NLLB Team)** (2022). *No Language Left Behind: Scaling Human-Centered MT*. arXiv.
- **Kudugunta, S., et al.** (2023). *MADLAD-400: A Multilingual And Document-Level Large Audited Dataset*. NeurIPS Datasets & Benchmarks.
- **Nguyen, T., et al.** (2023). *CulturaX: A Cleaned, Enormous, and Multilingual Dataset for LLMs*. (Often cited as Nguyen et al. 2023 / 2024.)
- **ImaniGooghari, A., et al.** (2023). *Glot500: Scaling Multilingual Corpora and Language Models to 500 Languages*. ACL.

## Language-ID
- **Kargaran, A., Imani, A., Yvon, F., Schütze, H.** (2023). *GlotLID: Language Identification for Low-Resource Languages*. EMNLP.
- **Joulin, A., et al.** (2017). *Bag of Tricks for Efficient Text Classification* (FastText). EACL.

## Deduplication
- **Lee, K., et al.** (2022). *Deduplicating Training Data Makes Language Models Better*. ACL.
- **Penedo, G., et al.** (2023). *The RefinedWeb Dataset for Falcon LLM*. NeurIPS — large-scale dedup methodology.

- **Carlini, N., et al.** (2023). *Quantifying Memorization Across Neural Language Models*. ICLR.
