# Canonical Sources — linguistic-lexicon

Snapshot 2026-04-23.

## Canonical practical lexicography
- **Atkins, B. T. S., & Rundell, M.** (2008). *The Oxford Guide to Practical Lexicography*. OUP. — THE canonical reference.
- **Hartmann, R. R. K.** (2001). *Teaching and Researching Lexicography*. Pearson.
- **Hartmann, R. R. K., & James, G.** (1998). *Dictionary of Lexicography*. Routledge.

## Theory
- **Geeraerts, D.** (2010). *Theories of Lexical Semantics*. OUP.
- **Cruse, D. A.** (1986). *Lexical Semantics*. Cambridge.
- **Pustejovsky, J.** (1995). *The Generative Lexicon*. MIT Press.

## Cross-lingual lexical resources
- **Wiktionary**: https://www.wiktionary.org/
- **DBnary** (Wiktionary as RDF): http://kaiko.getalp.org/about-dbnary/
- **PanLex**: https://panlex.org/
- **MUSE bilingual dictionaries**: https://github.com/facebookresearch/MUSE
- **Lexibank**: https://lexibank.clld.org/
