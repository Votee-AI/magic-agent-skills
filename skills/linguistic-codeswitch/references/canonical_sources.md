# Canonical Sources — linguistic-codeswitch

Snapshot 2026-04-23.

## Foundational
- **Myers-Scotton, C.** (1993). *Duelling Languages: Grammatical Structure in Codeswitching*. OUP. — Origin of Matrix Language Frame.
- **Poplack, S.** (1980). *Sometimes I'll start a sentence in Spanish y termino en español: Toward a typology of code-switching*. Linguistics 18.
- **Auer, P.** (ed.) (1998). *Code-switching in Conversation*. Routledge.

## NLP-side
- **Solorio, T., et al.** (2014). *Overview for the First Shared Task on Language Identification in Code-Switched Data*. EMNLP.
- **Aguilar, G., et al.** (2020). *LinCE: A Centralized Benchmark for Linguistic Code-switching Evaluation*. LREC.
- **Khanuja, S., et al.** (2020). *GLUECoS: An Evaluation Benchmark for Code-Switched NLP*. ACL.
- **Bouamor, H., et al.** (2018, 2019). *MADAR Corpus + shared task* (Arabic dialects). LREC + WANLP.
- **Diab, M., et al.** (2019). *DART corpus and shared task on dialect Arabic*.

## Surveys
- **Pratapa, A., Choudhury, M., Sitaram, S.** (2018). *Word Embeddings for Code-Mixed Language Processing*. EMNLP.
- **Sitaram, S., et al.** (2019). *A Survey of Code-switched Speech and Language Processing*. arXiv.
