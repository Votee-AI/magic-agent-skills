# Canonical Sources — linguistic-discourse

Snapshot 2026-04-23.

## Foundational textbook
- **Stede, M.** (2011). *Discourse Processing*. Morgan & Claypool. — **The** compact reference; framework-comparison stance.

## Discourse frameworks

### RST
- **Mann, W. C., & Thompson, S. A.** (1988). *Rhetorical Structure Theory: Toward a functional theory of text organization*. Text.
- **Carlson, L., Marcu, D., Okurowski, M. E.** (2001). *Building a discourse-tagged corpus in the framework of Rhetorical Structure Theory*. SIGDIAL.

### PDTB
- **Prasad, R., et al.** (2008). *The Penn Discourse Treebank 2.0*. LREC.
- **Webber, B., Prasad, R., Lee, A., Joshi, A.** (2019). *The Penn Discourse Treebank 3.0 Annotation Manual*. UPenn LDC.

### SDRT
- **Asher, N., & Lascarides, A.** (2003). *Logics of Conversation*. Cambridge University Press.

### GUM
- **Zeldes, A.** (2017). *The GUM corpus: creating multilayer resources in the classroom*. LREC.
- GUM: https://gucorpling.org/gum/

### Cross-framework + DISRPT
- **Braud, C., et al.** (2023, 2025). *DISRPT shared task on discourse parsing*. (Multiple proceedings.)

## Coreference
- **Pradhan, S., et al.** (2012). *CoNLL-2012 Shared Task: Modeling Multilingual Unrestricted Coreference in OntoNotes*. CoNLL.
- **Pradhan, S., et al.** (2022, 2023). *CRAC Shared Task on Multilingual Coreference Resolution*. CRAC workshop.
- **Dobrovoljc, K., et al.** (2017). *The Universal Anaphora Annotation Scheme*.
- **Wiseman, S., et al.** (2016). *Learning Global Features for Coreference Resolution*. NAACL.

## Discourse and pragmatics (broader)
- **Tannen, D., Hamilton, H., Schiffrin, D.** (eds.) (2015). *The Handbook of Discourse Analysis* (2nd ed.). Wiley.
- **Bender, E. M., & Lascarides, A.** (2019). *Linguistic Fundamentals for NLP II: 100 Essentials from Semantics and Pragmatics*. Morgan & Claypool. — Chapters on discourse + pragmatics.
- **Brown, G., & Yule, G.** (1983). *Discourse Analysis*. Cambridge.

## Coherence eval for LLMs (recent)
- **Karpinska, M., Akoury, N., Iyyer, M.** (2024). *One Thousand and One Pairs* (long-context novel reading benchmark).
- **Tian, K., et al.** (2024). *Verifying citation accuracy of LLMs on RAG*. arXiv.

## Refresh
- DISRPT: ~biennial.
- CRAC: annual.
- LLM coherence-eval: rapid; check ACL/EMNLP/NeurIPS annually.
