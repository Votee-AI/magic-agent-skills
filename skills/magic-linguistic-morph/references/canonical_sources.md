# Canonical Sources — magic-linguistic-morph

## UniMorph
- **Kirov, C., et al.** (2018). *UniMorph 2.0: Universal Morphology*. LREC.
- **Batsuren, K., et al.** (2022). *UniMorph 4.0: Universal Morphology*. LREC. — Current.

## SIGMORPHON
- SIGMORPHON shared task proceedings (annual; 2016-present).
- **Vylomova, E., et al.** (2020). *SIGMORPHON 2020 Shared Task on Multilingual Grapheme-to-Phoneme Conversion*. SIGMORPHON workshop.
- **Wiemerslage, A., et al.** (2023). *SIGMORPHON 2023 Shared Task on Interlinear Glossing*.
- **Goldman, O., et al.** (2023). *Morphological Inflection: A Reality Check*. ACL.

## FST toolkits
- **Lindén, K., et al.** (2009). *HFST Tools for Morphology*. Workshop on Systems and Frameworks for Computational Morphology.
- **Hulden, M.** (2009). *Foma: a finite-state compiler and library*. EACL demo.
- **Beesley, K. R., & Karttunen, L.** (2003). *Finite State Morphology*. CSLI Publications.
- HFST: https://hfst.github.io/
- foma: https://fomafst.github.io/
- Apertium: https://github.com/apertium

## Augmentation
- **Bergmanis, T., et al.** (2017). *Training Data Augmentation for Low-Resource Morphological Inflection*. SIGMORPHON.
- **Anastasopoulos, A., & Neubig, G.** (2019). *Pushing the Limits of Low-Resource Morphological Inflection*. EMNLP.
- **Faruqui, M., et al.** (2016). *Morphological Inflection Generation Using Character Sequence to Sequence Learning*. NAACL.

## Foundational morphology textbooks
- **Booij, G.** (2007). *The Grammar of Words: An Introduction to Linguistic Morphology*. OUP.
- **Hippisley, A., & Stump, G.** (eds.) (2016). *The Cambridge Handbook of Morphology*. Cambridge University Press.
- **Aronoff, M., & Fudeman, K.** (2011). *What is Morphology?* (2nd ed.). Wiley-Blackwell.
